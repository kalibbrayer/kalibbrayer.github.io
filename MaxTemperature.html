Latitude vs. Max Temperature


The figure above plots the observed maximum temperature (y-axis) against the latitude of each city (x-axis). This scatter plot shows a fairly clear relationship between temperature and latitude. The maximum temperature seems to peak near the equator where latitude = 0. Cities in the northern hemisphere seems to have cooler maximum temperatures than cities in the southern hemisphere at similar distanes from the equator.
